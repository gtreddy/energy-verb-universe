### The Recursive Mechanism

The universe builds itself through recursion.
A pattern repeats and integrates to form a higher level of organization.
Complexity arises because the "Energy Verb" operates within stable loops.
There is no need for a new force; each layer is built from the primitives of the layer below.

### Layer 1: Energy Flows into Stable Information

When the **Energy Verb** toggles between Mode 1 (Kinetic) and Mode 0 (Potential), it eventually finds a stable path.
Mathematics provides the constraints that prevent the energy from dissipating.
This stabilized, constrained flow is what we call **Information**.
Information is the "memory" of the action.

### Layer 2: Information into Patterns

Information does not stay isolated.
Stable informational loops cluster together to form **Patterns**.
In the micro-world, these are atoms and molecules.
In the macro-world, these are stars, galaxies, and crystals.
These are architectures without intention.
They exist simply because some configurations are mathematically stable and others are not.

### Layer 3: Patterns into Self-Maintaining Systems (Life)

Life is not a miracle; it is a higher-order recursive pattern.
It occurs when informational loops become self-maintaining.
Biology does not violate physics; it exploits it to preserve flow patterns across time.
Reproduction is the continuity of the pattern, not the creation of something new.

### Layer 4: Life into Predictive Structures (Brains)

When self-maintaining systems need to regulate energy flow, they develop boundaries and sensors.
Neurons integrate these signals.
A brain emerges when these neurons form large recurrent networks.
The brain is a "pattern of patterns".
It functions to predict the environment so the "Energy Verb" can continue to flow efficiently.
